Some interesting things to do in Wilmington

Attend a show or concert

The Murphy Theater is a historic venue that has hosted legends like Elvis Presley, Johnny Cash, and more!

Visit historic landmarks

Featuring a grand marble staircase and a 32ft dome, The Clinton County Courthouse was built in 1919 and cost $370,000.

The Ohio Renaissance Festival

The Ohio Renaissance Festival is an amazing experience. Take a step back into simpler times, enjoy a pint with friends and take in a joust!
