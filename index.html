Welcome to Wilmington, OH

Where is Wilmington?

Wilmington, Ohio is located approximately 45 minutes northeast of Cincinnati, and 60 minutes southwest of Columbus. This small town has a major role in the shaping of Ohio as we know it.

Since Wilmington is between the booming river-town of Cincinnati and the State Capitol - many enterpreneurs and politicians had to travel through Wilmington on regular occasions. Interstate 71 runs directly through Wilmington on it's way between Cincy and Columbus.

What is Wilmington known for?

Wilmington is probably most known for the air park. Once owned by Airborn Express, and DHL, Wilmington Air Park is now home to Amazon distribution. Being in the center of two of Ohio's largest cities makes Wilmington a fantastic location for a shipping hub.

Wilmington is also known for "The national weather service in Wilmington, Ohio has issued a Severe Thunderstorm Warning for...." That's right - one of the most accurate doppler radar towers, as well as the national weather service, are located in this forgotten rural town.
